Our History

Fruitful Youth Church has a rich history of serving our community and spreading the gospel. Our focus is on making disciples and building Christ-centered communities.

We believe in equipping believers to live out their faith and become leaders. Our mission is to break the spirit of inferiority, cultivate excellence, and advance God's end-time agenda.

Our Core Beliefs and Vision

Make Disciples

Commit individuals to Christ's teachings

Build Churches

Establish communities proclaiming Christ

Marks of a Disciple

Learn from Christ, led by the Spirit, live for God, lead others to Christ

Marks of a Church

Teach doctrine, engage in prayer/worship, foster fellowship, evangelize

Our Message

We proclaim the gospel of salvation, acceptance, strength, healing, liberty, hope, prosperity, and abundant life in Christ.
