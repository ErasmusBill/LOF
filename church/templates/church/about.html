Our Journey

Walking in faith through the generations

Foundation

Gospel Light International Church

Under the visionary leadership of Bishop Matthew Addae Mensah, GLIC was established with a divine mandate to raise disciples who would impact nations. Since its inception, the church has been dedicated to creating a Christ-centered community that nurtures spiritual growth and development. Guided by strong biblical principles and a passion for evangelism, Bishop Mensah has continually inspired the congregation to pursue lives of purpose and service. With a focus on discipling and empowering believers, GLIC has become a transformative force, raising leaders who are equipped to bring hope, healing, and positive change to communities around the world. From local outreach programs to international missions, the church has expanded its vision and influence, all in pursuit of spreading the light of the Gospel to all corners of the earth.

Transition

Land Of Fruitfulness

Through divine direction, Fruitful Youth emerged from the Land of Fruitfulness, marking a significant milestone in our journey. Led by Bishop and Lady Bishop Dodoo Amoo, this ministry was birthed with a mission to inspire, empower, and guide young people toward a path of purpose and spiritual maturity. Rooted in faith and led by strong values, Fruitful Youth is more than just a group; it is a movement that seeks to equip the next generation with the tools they need to navigate life's challenges and fulfill their God-given potential. From dynamic worship sessions to transformative teachings and impactful community outreach, Fruitful Youth is dedicated to nurturing young minds and hearts to become leaders and positive influencers in their communities and beyond. With every gathering, we witness lives being transformed, faith deepening, and a new generation rising to embrace their call to make a difference.

Present

Fruitful Youth

Today, under the entire leadership of Gospel Light International Church, we stand as a beacon of hope for young people, guiding them toward a future rooted in faith, integrity, and purpose. Through mentorship, dynamic worship, and impactful teachings, we are committed to addressing the unique challenges facing today’s youth, offering them a safe space to grow spiritually and personally. Our programs encourage young individuals to embrace their potential, find their identity in Christ, and take bold steps to influence their communities positively. As we continue to expand our outreach and deepen our impact, we are seeing lives transformed, minds renewed, and a new generation prepared to carry forward a legacy of faith and excellence. With a vision to ignite passion and purpose in the hearts of every young person, we are building a foundation of hope that will resonate across generations.
